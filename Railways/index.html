Railways



  METRO

        Metro trains are a type of urban mass transit system designed to efficiently transport passengers within cities and metropolitan areas. They are characterized by frequent stops, short distances between stations, and high-frequency services. Metro systems often have dedicated tracks, tunnels, or elevated tracks that allow them to operate independently of other traffic.


  SEATING

       Seating trains, also known as conventional trains or day trains, are designed for medium to long-distance travel and offer various types of seating arrangements for passengers. These trains are suitable for journeys that can be completed within a single day without the need for overnight accommodation


  SLEEPER

       Sleeper trains are designed for long-distance travel, often spanning overnight journeys. These trains provide sleeping accommodations for passengers, allowing them to rest and sleep during the trip. Sleeper trains are particularly popular for routes that cover vast distances and provide an efficient way to travel while saving on hotel expenses.
